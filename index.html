How I work

I go in as one senior operator — not a team. I diagnose quickly, in days or weeks rather than months. I deliver a plain-English read on what is actually wrong, what to fix first, and who needs to do it. Where it helps, I stay on to do the work with your team.

No juniors on the file. No discovery phase dressed up as a project. No deck factory. No generic frameworks.

The view I work from

People do not fail, systems do.

Most performance problems are the predictable output of how work is organised, how decisions get made, and how accountability is set. Fix that base, and results follow. Layer tooling on top of a weak base, and nothing lasts.

That is why so many AI, ERP and transformation programmes stall. The technology is rarely the real problem.
